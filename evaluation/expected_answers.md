# CivicAI Expected Answers (Evaluation Guide)

## Q1 — Policy ADM-1.5.3
**Question:** What does the document say about Policy ADM-1.5.3?

**Minimum correct answer must include:**
- Mentions “ADM-1.5.3”
- States that it establishes policies/procedures/forms for amending the Comprehensive Plan
- Mentions publication in a development procedures manual/handbook accessible to the public

**Correct reference should:**
- Cite a snippet that contains “Policy ADM-1.5.3” AND its descriptive sentence (not ADM-1.4.x)

**Incorrect if:**
- Cites ADM-1.4.2 or unrelated PRP policies
- Answers without showing evidence that includes ADM-1.5.3 text

**Gold snippet (from doc):**
> Policy ADM-1.5.3. Establish policies, procedures, and forms for amending the Comprehensive Plan...
